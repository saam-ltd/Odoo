Odoo Direct Print to Printer

Introducing our innovative report printing app that offers seamless functionality and user-friendly features. With our app, you can easily print reports directly without the hassle of downloading them as PDF files. Simply select the "Print" option, and your reports will be sent directly to the printer, saving you time and effort. In addition to printing, we have included a dedicated "Download" button alongside the "Print" option allowing you to directly download reports without going through the printing process. The best part is that our app requires no additional configuration or the need for any third-party applications.
